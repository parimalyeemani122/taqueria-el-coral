Who We Are

The Heart of El Coral

Taqueria El Coral is a family-run Mexican restaurant with deep roots in San Jose, California. What started as a dream to bring the bold, honest flavors of traditional Mexican cooking to our community has grown into two beloved locations — and a lot of happy regulars.

Every taco, burrito, and plate is made the way it's always been made: fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and the kind of care you can only find in a family kitchen. We choose our meats, prepare our salsas, and cook our beans the same way every single day.

"We cook the same way our family has cooked for generations — nothing shortcut, nothing frozen, nothing fake."

Whether you're stopping in for a quick breakfast burrito before work or settling in for a full carne asada platter with your familia on a weekend, you'll find the same warmth, the same quality, and the same flavors every time you walk through our doors.

Twelve meats. Ten menu categories. Hundreds of possible combinations. And every one of them made fresh, made fast, and made with love.
